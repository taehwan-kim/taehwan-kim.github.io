About

I am a Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley in EECS working with Vladimir Stojanović in the Berkeley Wireless Research Center (BWRC).

I enjoy designing and analyzing complex systems, and also improving the way people design and analyze such systems. As a member of Integrated Systems Group, I am trying to tackle these problems in the context of integrated opto-electronics. I am currently working on the design of free-space optical systems such as LIDAR and communication links based on integrated electronics-photonics platform.
